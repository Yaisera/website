About Me

I’m Alex Bessin, a photographer with a strong focus on sports, nature, and portrait photography. I enjoy capturing movement, emotion, and real moments—whether it’s the intensity of competition, the quiet detail of the outdoors, or the personality of an individual.

I’m constantly working to refine my style and technical skills, aiming to create images that feel authentic, dynamic, and visually strong.

Please reach out regarding any potential projects or collaborations. Located in Coeur d'Alene, ID
