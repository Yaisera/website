About Me

I’m Alex Bessin, a photographer with a strong interest in sports, nature, and portrait photography. I enjoy capturing movement, emotion, and authentic moments—whether it’s the intensity of competition, the detail of the outdoors, or the personality of an individual.

Sports photography fuels my passion for timing and storytelling, while nature photography keeps me connected to light, texture, and atmosphere. Portraits allow me to slow down and focus on expression and character.

I’m constantly refining my technical skills and creative style to produce images that feel dynamic, natural, and visually strong.
